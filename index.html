About

The Broadway Food Festival is an annual event that takes place on the village green in Broadway on the second Sunday in September. Artisan producers will be displaying their products with a wide variety of hot and cold food and drink available to purchase. There is also a marquee with a selection of demonstrations taking place throughout the day. It is run every year by a committee of volunteers with the current team pictured below. The event is funded by Wychavon from the 'levelling up' grant.
